Milkshake

One of the best beverages for a hot summer!

Let's try making one!

Ingredients:

  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Milk
  • Whipped toppings
  • Sprinkles
  • Maraschino cherry

Steps:

  1. In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk.
  2. Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry.
