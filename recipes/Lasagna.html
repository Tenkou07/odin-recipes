Lasagna

One of the best italian dishes!

Tender sheets of pasta, a cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce make the perfect dish!

Ingredients:

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat Sauce

Steps:

  1. Mix the ricotta and cheeses (per recipe below) with an egg in a bowl, and set aside. No ricotta? No problem, cottage cheese works just fine in this recipe!
  2. Brown the onion, garlic, and meat in a pot on the stovetop. Add the pasta sauce and tomato paste & simmer a few minutes.
  3. Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with noodles and bake until bubbling
